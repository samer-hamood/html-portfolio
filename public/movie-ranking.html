The Best Movies According to Samer

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Dark Knight

My favourite movie due to its non-stop action and efficiency.

Avengers: Infinity War

Close second, the culmination of the overarching narrative of the previous MCU movies forming the Infinity saga. Villain-centric, the movie focuses on the antagonist that it introduces.

Captain America: Civil War

Non-conventional story, having the heroes fighting each other.
